Best Free Productivity Chrome Extensions (with Optional Pro Upgrades)

Your browser is where you spend most of your working hours. Yet most people run it completely stock, missing out on tools that could save them thirty minutes or more every day. The best part? You do not need to pay anything to get started.

Here are nine Chrome extensions that genuinely improve daily productivity. Each one is completely free, with an optional Pro upgrade available for a one-time payment of just 1.49 EUR -- no subscriptions, no recurring charges, no data harvesting.

Focus and Time Management

Focus Guard Free + Pro 1.49 EUR

Block distracting websites during work sessions. Set custom blocklists, schedule focus periods, and track how much deep work time you actually achieve each day. The free version covers unlimited sites and basic scheduling. Pro adds detailed analytics, custom schedules per day of the week, and a strict mode that cannot be easily bypassed.

Tab Hero Free + Pro 1.49 EUR

If you are the kind of person who runs 40 tabs at once, Tab Hero is your rescue. It groups, suspends, and organizes tabs automatically. Search across all open tabs instantly, save tab sessions for later, and reclaim the memory that Chrome loves to devour. Pro adds tab analytics and cross-device session sync.

Reading and Research

Reading List Pro Free + Pro 1.49 EUR

Save articles to read later without cluttering your bookmarks. Reading List Pro strips away ads and distractions, presenting a clean reading view. Organize saved articles with tags and folders. Pro adds offline reading and full-text search across your entire saved library.

Markdown Clipper Free + Pro 1.49 EUR

Clip any web page or selection as clean Markdown. Perfect for developers and writers who live in Markdown-based tools like Obsidian, Notion, or GitHub. One click converts any article into perfectly formatted Markdown, preserving headings, links, code blocks, and images. Pro adds batch clipping and custom templates.

Monitoring and Utilities

PageWatch Free + Pro 1.49 EUR

Monitor any web page for changes and get notified instantly. Track price drops, stock availability, job postings, or competitor updates. Set check intervals from every minute to once a day. Pro adds visual change highlighting and email notifications.

InvoiceBlitz Free + Pro 1.49 EUR

Create professional invoices directly in your browser in under a minute. Fill in your details, add line items, and export as PDF. No account needed, no cloud upload required -- everything stays on your machine. Pro adds custom branding, recurring invoice templates, and multi-currency support.

QR Code Generator Pro Free + Pro 1.49 EUR

Generate QR codes for any URL, text, WiFi credentials, or contact card with one click. Customize colors and size, then download as PNG or SVG. Pro adds logo embedding, batch generation, and analytics tracking.

Media and Organization

Video Speed Pro Free + Pro 1.49 EUR

Control playback speed on any HTML5 video player -- YouTube, Vimeo, Udemy, or any other site. Go from 0.1x to 16x with keyboard shortcuts. The time you save watching tutorials and lectures at 1.5x to 2x adds up to hours every week. Pro adds per-site speed memory and an always-visible speed overlay.

Bookmark Sorter Free + Pro 1.49 EUR

Automatically sort and organize your bookmarks by name, date, or URL. Remove duplicates, find broken bookmark links, and clean up years of accumulated clutter in a few clicks. Pro adds scheduled auto-sorting and folder merge suggestions.

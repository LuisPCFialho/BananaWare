Privacy Policy — CareerTracker

Last updated: March 2026  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

CareerTracker automatically detects when you apply for jobs and helps you track your applications. All data is stored locally on your device — never on external servers.

2. Data We Collect

Stored locally on your device only:

  • Job application details you choose to track: company name, job title, URL, application date, status, notes, salary information
  • Your settings and reminder preferences

This data is collected from job listing pages that you visit and explicitly apply to, or that you manually add.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your login credentials on job sites
  • Your resume or cover letter content
  • Any browsing activity on sites unrelated to job applications
  • Personal identifiers beyond what you voluntarily enter

4. Permissions Explained

  • storage — saves your job application tracker locally
  • notifications / alarms — sends follow-up reminders you set
  • tabs / host permissions — detects job application pages on LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, Greenhouse, Lever, Workday. Only activates on these sites

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties.

6. Data Deletion

Delete individual applications or all data from the extension popup at any time.

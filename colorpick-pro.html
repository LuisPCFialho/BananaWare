Privacy Policy — ColorPick Pro

Last updated: March 2026  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

ColorPick Pro extracts color palettes from webpages. Color data is processed locally and never transmitted to external servers.

2. Data We Collect

Stored locally only: color palettes you choose to save, and your settings. Colors extracted from pages are processed in-browser and not stored unless you explicitly click "Save Palette".

3. Permissions Explained

  • activeTab / scripting — reads computed styles from the current page when you click Extract
  • storage — saves your palettes and settings locally
  • clipboardWrite — copies color values to your clipboard when you click Copy
  • downloads — exports palettes as CSS/JSON files

4. Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.

Privacy Policy — ContractGuard

Last updated: March 2026  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

ContractGuard is a Chrome Extension that scans webpages for risky legal clauses and highlights them. All processing happens entirely within your browser — no text is ever sent to external servers.

2. Data We Collect

ContractGuard stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your custom risk patterns (text strings you define)
  • Scan results per URL (counts of HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW findings) for displaying in the popup
  • Your settings (auto-scan toggle, highlight style)

The text content of webpages you scan is processed entirely in your browser and is never transmitted anywhere.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • The text or content of any contracts or documents you scan
  • Your name, email, or personal identifiers
  • Browsing history

4. Permissions Explained

  • storage — saves your custom patterns and settings locally
  • activeTab / host permissions (<all_urls>) — needed to inject the highlight overlay on any page you choose to scan. The extension only activates when you click "Scan" or use the context menu
  • contextMenus — adds a right-click "Scan with ContractGuard" option

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties.

6. Data Deletion

Clear all data via the extension popup or by uninstalling the extension.

Privacy Policy — Dark Mode Everywhere

Last updated: March 2025  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

Dark Mode Everywhere is a Chrome Extension that forces a dark mode on any website using CSS inversion and custom overrides. You can enable dark mode globally or per-site, and schedule it to activate automatically. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

Dark Mode Everywhere stores the following data locally on your device only using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API:

  • Your list of enabled/disabled sites (domain names only — no page content or paths)
  • Your dark mode settings (inversion level, brightness, contrast, sepia sliders)
  • Your scheduling rules (time of day and days of week for automatic activation)
  • Your Pro license key (if applicable), stored locally to verify activation status

No browsing data or page content is ever sent to external servers. All preferences are stored locally on your device.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • The full URL paths or content of pages you visit
  • Browsing history
  • Any information about page content — dark mode is applied via CSS only

4. LemonSqueezy License Validation (Pro)

If you purchase a Pro license, your license key is sent to LemonSqueezy (our payment processor) solely to verify its validity. No other data is included in this request. LemonSqueezy's privacy policy is available at lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

5. Permissions Explained

  • storage — saves your site list, slider settings, and schedule locally
  • scripting / <all_urls> — injects the dark mode CSS stylesheet into pages you visit (based on your per-site settings)
  • alarms — powers the scheduling feature to turn dark mode on/off at set times

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. The only external network call made by this extension is the LemonSqueezy license validation described in Section 4.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can clear your site list and settings from the extension popup, or delete all data by uninstalling the extension.

8. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

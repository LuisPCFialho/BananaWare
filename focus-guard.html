Privacy Policy — Focus Guard

Last updated: March 2025  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

Focus Guard is a Chrome Extension that combines a Pomodoro timer with a website blocker to help you stay focused. You define which sites to block during focus sessions, and the extension enforces those rules locally. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

Focus Guard stores the following data locally on your device only using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API:

  • Your list of blocked websites (domain names you choose to block)
  • Pomodoro session history (start time, duration, session type — focus or break)
  • Your timer and scheduling settings (work duration, break duration, schedule rules)
  • Your Pro license key (if applicable), stored locally to verify activation status

No browsing data is ever sent to external servers. All blocking rules and session history stay on your device.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • The content of pages you visit or attempt to visit
  • Browsing history outside of the session logs you generate
  • Any data about sites that are not on your block list

4. LemonSqueezy License Validation (Pro)

If you purchase a Pro license, your license key is sent to LemonSqueezy (our payment processor) solely to verify its validity. No other data is included in this request. LemonSqueezy's privacy policy is available at lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

5. Permissions Explained

  • storage — saves your block list, session history, and timer settings locally
  • alarms — manages the Pomodoro countdown and scheduled blocking periods
  • notifications — alerts you when a focus session starts, ends, or a break is over
  • declarativeNetRequest — enforces website blocking rules without reading page content
  • tabs — redirects blocked URLs to the Focus Guard block page

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. The only external network call made by this extension is the LemonSqueezy license validation described in Section 4.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can clear your session history and block list from the extension settings, or delete all data by uninstalling the extension.

8. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

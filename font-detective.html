Privacy Policy — FontDetective

Last updated: March 2026  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

FontDetective identifies fonts used on webpages. All font detection is done locally by reading computed CSS styles — no data is sent to external servers.

2. Data We Collect

Stored locally only: your inspection history (last 10 elements) and your settings. Font names are not stored unless you export them.

3. Permissions Explained

  • activeTab / scripting — reads computed font styles from the current page
  • storage — saves inspection history and settings locally
  • clipboardWrite — copies CSS snippets when you click Copy
  • contextMenus — adds "Inspect font here" to the right-click menu

4. Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.

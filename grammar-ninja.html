Privacy Policy — Grammar Ninja

Last updated: March 2025  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

Grammar Ninja is a Chrome Extension that checks grammar and spelling in real-time across all text fields in your browser — with no external API calls. All language processing runs locally in your browser using a bundled grammar engine. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

Grammar Ninja stores the following data locally on your device only using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API:

  • Your personal dictionary (custom words you choose to add)
  • Your settings and preferences (language, check aggressiveness, tone detection rules)
  • A local counter for daily check usage (Free tier limit enforcement — resets daily, never sent externally)
  • Your Pro license key (if applicable), stored locally to verify activation status

Text you type is NEVER sent to external servers. Grammar analysis is performed entirely within the browser using a bundled local engine. Your writing stays private.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • The text content you type into any text field
  • Documents, emails, or messages you compose
  • Browsing history or page URLs

4. LemonSqueezy License Validation (Pro)

If you purchase a Pro license, your license key is sent to LemonSqueezy (our payment processor) solely to verify its validity. No other data — and crucially, no text you have typed — is included in this request. LemonSqueezy's privacy policy is available at lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

5. Permissions Explained

  • storage — saves your custom dictionary and settings locally
  • scripting / <all_urls> — injects the grammar checker overlay into text fields on any page you visit (the text is analyzed in-browser and never leaves your device)
  • contextMenus — adds grammar-fix suggestions to the right-click menu on text fields

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. The only external network call made by this extension is the LemonSqueezy license validation described in Section 4.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can clear your custom dictionary and reset settings from the extension popup, or delete all data by uninstalling the extension.

8. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

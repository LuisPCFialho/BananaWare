Privacy Policy — InvoiceBlitz

Last updated: March 2026  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

InvoiceBlitz is an invoice generator that works entirely within your browser. Your invoices and client data are stored only on your device — never transmitted to external servers.

2. Data We Collect

Stored locally only: your business profile (name, company, address), client information you enter, and invoice records.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Payment information or bank details
  • Any data from websites you visit

4. Permissions Explained

  • storage — saves your invoices, profile, and settings locally
  • downloads — downloads generated invoice files to your computer

5. Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties. Your invoices stay on your device.

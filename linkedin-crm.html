Privacy Policy — LinkedInCRM

Last updated: March 2026  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

LinkedInCRM adds a lightweight CRM overlay to LinkedIn profile pages. All contact data you save is stored locally on your device only — never on external servers.

2. Data We Collect

Stored locally on your device only:

  • Contact information from LinkedIn profiles you explicitly save: name, job title, company (read from the public page), LinkedIn profile URL
  • Notes, tags, and follow-up reminders you add manually
  • Outreach status you set manually

Data is only collected when you actively click "Save" on a profile. We do not scrape LinkedIn automatically or in bulk.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • LinkedIn login credentials or session tokens
  • Private messages or connection data
  • Any data from pages other than linkedin.com

4. Permissions Explained

  • storage — saves your CRM contacts locally
  • notifications / alarms — sends follow-up reminders you set
  • host permissions (linkedin.com) — injects the CRM panel only on LinkedIn profile pages

5. Relationship with LinkedIn

LinkedInCRM is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to LinkedIn Corporation. It reads only publicly visible profile information.

6. Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.

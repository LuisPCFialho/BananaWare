Privacy Policy — MeetingMeter

Last updated: March 2026  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

MeetingMeter is a Chrome Extension that calculates the real-time cost of meetings on Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and Zoom. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

MeetingMeter stores the following data locally on your device only using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API:

  • Meeting duration, attendee count, platform name, and calculated cost per meeting
  • Your settings (average salary, currency, work hours per year)

We do not collect, transmit, or store any data on external servers. All data stays on your device.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • The content of your meetings or conversations
  • Browsing history outside of meeting platforms
  • Any information from websites other than Google Meet, Teams, and Zoom

4. Permissions Explained

  • storage — saves your settings and meeting history locally on your device
  • notifications — shows a desktop notification when a meeting ends with the cost summary
  • tabs / alarms — detects when you are on a meeting page
  • Host permissions (meet.google.com, teams.microsoft.com, zoom.us) — injects the cost ticker overlay only on these sites

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. Ever.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time by clicking "Clear history" in the extension popup, or by uninstalling the extension.

7. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

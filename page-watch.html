Privacy Policy — PageWatch

Last updated: March 2026  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

PageWatch monitors webpages for changes and sends you desktop notifications when content updates. All data is stored locally on your device.

2. Data We Collect

Stored locally on your device only:

  • URLs of pages you choose to watch
  • Page content snapshots (text content used for change comparison)
  • Change history (timestamp and summary of detected changes)
  • Your watch settings (check interval, notification preferences)

Page snapshots are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to external servers.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identifiers of any kind
  • Browsing history of pages you have not explicitly added to PageWatch
  • Login credentials or form data

4. Permissions Explained

  • storage — stores your watches and snapshots locally
  • alarms — schedules periodic checks in the background
  • notifications — delivers desktop alerts when changes are detected
  • scripting / tabs — fetches page content for comparison when an alarm fires
  • host permissions (<all_urls>) — needed to read content from any URL you add to your watch list

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties.

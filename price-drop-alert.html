Privacy Policy — Price Drop Alert

Last updated: March 2025  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

Price Drop Alert is a Chrome Extension that lets you track Amazon product prices and notifies you via desktop notification when a price drops. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

Price Drop Alert stores the following data locally on your device only using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API:

  • Amazon product URLs and ASINs you choose to track
  • Recorded price history (price value, currency, and timestamp) for each tracked product
  • Your alert threshold settings (target price or percentage drop)
  • Your Pro license key (if applicable), stored locally to verify activation status

No product tracking data is ever sent to our servers. All price history and alert rules stay on your device.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your Amazon account credentials or order history
  • Browsing history outside of Amazon product pages you have explicitly added
  • Payment information of any kind

4. LemonSqueezy License Validation (Pro)

If you purchase a Pro license, your license key is sent to LemonSqueezy (our payment processor) solely to verify its validity. No other data is included in this request. LemonSqueezy's privacy policy is available at lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

5. Permissions Explained

  • storage — saves your tracked products, price history, and settings locally
  • alarms — schedules periodic price checks in the background
  • notifications — delivers desktop alerts when a price drop is detected
  • scripting / tabs — reads the current price from Amazon product pages
  • host permissions (amazon.com and regional variants) — required to fetch price data from the Amazon pages you track

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. The only external network call made by this extension is the LemonSqueezy license validation described in Section 4.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can remove individual tracked products at any time from the extension popup, or delete all data by uninstalling the extension.

8. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

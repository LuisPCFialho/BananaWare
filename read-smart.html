Privacy Policy — ReadSmart

Last updated: March 2026  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

ReadSmart analyzes article content to extract reading time, key sentences, and key terms. All analysis is done locally in your browser — no article text is ever sent to external servers.

2. Data We Collect

Stored locally only: your saved article library (title, URL, summary, key terms, date saved) and your settings. Article text is analyzed in-browser and not stored unless you click "Save to Library".

3. Permissions Explained

  • storage — saves your article library and settings
  • activeTab / host permissions — reads article text from the current page when you click Analyze
  • contextMenus — adds "Analyze with ReadSmart" to the right-click menu
  • downloads — exports your library as JSON or Markdown

4. Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.

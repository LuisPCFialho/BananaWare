Privacy Policy — Screenshot Pro

Last updated: March 2025  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

Screenshot Pro is a Chrome Extension that captures full-page screenshots and provides annotation tools including arrows, rectangles, text labels, and blur. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

Screenshot Pro stores the following data locally on your device only using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API:

  • Your annotation preferences and default settings (color, line thickness, font size)
  • Your Pro license key (if applicable), stored locally to verify activation status

Screenshots you capture are saved directly to your device via the browser's standard download mechanism. No screenshot image data is ever uploaded to our servers.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • The content of screenshots you take
  • Browsing history or the URLs of pages you screenshot
  • Any data from pages you visit that you have not explicitly chosen to screenshot

4. LemonSqueezy License Validation (Pro)

If you purchase a Pro license, your license key is sent to LemonSqueezy (our payment processor) solely to verify its validity. No other data is included in this request. LemonSqueezy's privacy policy is available at lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

5. Permissions Explained

  • storage — saves your annotation settings and preferences locally
  • activeTab — captures the visible area of the current tab when you click the extension button
  • scripting — injects the annotation overlay into the current page
  • downloads — saves the final screenshot image to your device
  • tabs / <all_urls> — required for full-page capture which scrolls and stitches the entire page

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. The only external network call made by this extension is the LemonSqueezy license validation described in Section 4.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All settings are stored locally. Screenshots are saved to your device's download folder and are under your full control. Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored settings.

8. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

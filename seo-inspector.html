Privacy Policy — SEO Inspector

Last updated: March 2025  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

SEO Inspector is a Chrome Extension that analyzes the current webpage for on-page SEO signals, provides a score, and gives actionable recommendations. All analysis is performed locally in your browser. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

SEO Inspector stores the following data locally on your device only using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API:

  • Audit history: URL, SEO score, and summary of recommendations for pages you have analyzed
  • Your settings and report export preferences
  • Your Pro license key (if applicable), stored locally to verify activation status

Page analysis (reading meta tags, headings, link structure, etc.) is done entirely in-browser. No page content or URL data is ever sent to our servers.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • The full HTML content of pages you analyze
  • Browsing history of pages you have not explicitly analyzed with the extension
  • Login credentials or form data

4. LemonSqueezy License Validation (Pro)

If you purchase a Pro license, your license key is sent to LemonSqueezy (our payment processor) solely to verify its validity. No other data is included in this request. LemonSqueezy's privacy policy is available at lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

5. Permissions Explained

  • storage — saves your audit history and settings locally
  • activeTab — reads the DOM of the current tab when you click "Analyze" to extract SEO signals
  • scripting — injects the analysis script into the page
  • downloads — exports audit reports as JSON or CSV files to your device

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. The only external network call made by this extension is the LemonSqueezy license validation described in Section 4.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can clear your audit history from the extension settings panel, or delete all data by uninstalling the extension.

8. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

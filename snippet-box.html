Privacy Policy — Snippet Box

Last updated: March 2025  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

Snippet Box is a Chrome Extension that lets you save reusable text snippets and insert them into any text field via a context menu or keyboard trigger shortcuts. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

Snippet Box stores the following data locally on your device only using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API:

  • The text snippets you create (title, content, trigger shortcut, and optional folder assignment)
  • Your folder/category structure (Pro feature)
  • Your settings and preferences (keyboard shortcut configuration, default insertion behavior)
  • Your Pro license key (if applicable), stored locally to verify activation status

Your snippet content never leaves your device. All data is stored locally and is never transmitted to external servers.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • The content of text fields on pages where you insert snippets
  • Browsing history or the URLs of pages you visit
  • Any data from pages where snippets are inserted, beyond the act of inserting them

4. LemonSqueezy License Validation (Pro)

If you purchase a Pro license, your license key is sent to LemonSqueezy (our payment processor) solely to verify its validity. No other data is included in this request. LemonSqueezy's privacy policy is available at lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

5. Permissions Explained

  • storage — saves your snippets, folders, and settings locally
  • contextMenus — adds "Insert Snippet" to the right-click context menu on text fields
  • scripting / activeTab — injects the snippet text into the focused text field when triggered

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. The only external network call made by this extension is the LemonSqueezy license validation described in Section 4.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete individual snippets or all data from the extension popup, or delete all data by uninstalling the extension.

8. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

Privacy Policy — Tab Hero

Last updated: March 2025  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

Tab Hero is a Chrome Extension that provides advanced tab management: search open tabs, save and restore sessions, group tabs by domain, and close duplicates. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

Tab Hero stores the following data locally on your device only using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API:

  • Saved session data: tab titles, URLs, and window layout for sessions you explicitly save
  • Tab usage statistics (open count, last accessed time) — used locally to power the stats dashboard
  • Your settings and preferences (sort order, grouping rules, etc.)
  • Your Pro license key (if applicable), stored locally to verify activation status

No tab data or browsing history is ever sent to external servers. Everything stays on your device.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • The content of any webpage you visit
  • Form inputs, passwords, or any sensitive page data
  • Browsing history beyond the tab metadata (title and URL) stored in sessions you explicitly save

4. LemonSqueezy License Validation (Pro)

If you purchase a Pro license, your license key is sent to LemonSqueezy (our payment processor) solely to verify its validity. No other data is included in this request. LemonSqueezy's privacy policy is available at lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

5. Permissions Explained

  • storage — saves your sessions, stats, and settings locally
  • tabs — reads open tab titles and URLs to power search, grouping, and duplicate detection
  • sessions — used to restore saved sessions
  • bookmarks (optional) — only accessed if you choose to export a session as bookmarks

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. The only external network call made by this extension is the LemonSqueezy license validation described in Section 4.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete individual sessions or all data from the extension settings panel, or by uninstalling the extension.

8. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

Privacy Policy — TableBlitz

Last updated: March 2026  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

TableBlitz detects HTML tables on webpages and lets you export them as CSV, TSV, or JSON. All data processing happens locally in your browser.

2. Data We Collect

TableBlitz stores only your preferences (default export format, delimiter choice, BOM setting) locally using chrome.storage.local. No table data is ever stored or transmitted.

3. How Table Data Is Handled

When you click "Export", table content is read from the current page, converted to your chosen format, and downloaded directly to your computer. The data never passes through any server — it is handled entirely within your browser.

4. Permissions Explained

  • activeTab / scripting — reads table data from the current page when you explicitly click Export
  • downloads — saves the exported file to your computer
  • storage — saves your format preferences
  • contextMenus — adds right-click export options on tables

5. Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties. Ever.

Privacy Policy — TimeZone Pal

Last updated: March 2025  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

TimeZone Pal is a Chrome Extension that displays a world clock for multiple timezones and includes a meeting planner to find overlapping working hours across locations. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

TimeZone Pal stores the following data locally on your device only using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API:

  • The list of timezones you have added to your world clock (timezone names/identifiers only)
  • Your display preferences (clock format, date format, theme)
  • Meeting planner saved configurations (list of timezone participants and preferred hours)
  • Your Pro license key (if applicable), stored locally to verify activation status

No data is ever sent to external servers. All timezone data is derived from your device's system clock and the industry-standard IANA timezone database bundled with the extension.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your physical location or IP address
  • Any browsing history or page content
  • Meeting content, calendar data, or contact information

4. LemonSqueezy License Validation (Pro)

If you purchase a Pro license, your license key is sent to LemonSqueezy (our payment processor) solely to verify its validity. No other data is included in this request. LemonSqueezy's privacy policy is available at lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

5. Permissions Explained

  • storage — saves your timezone list and settings locally

TimeZone Pal requires minimal permissions because all functionality runs entirely within the extension popup using your device's system clock.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. The only external network call made by this extension is the LemonSqueezy license validation described in Section 4.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can clear your timezone list and settings from the extension popup, or delete all data by uninstalling the extension.

8. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

Privacy Policy — Web Annotator

Last updated: March 2025  |  Developer: LuisPCFialho

1. Overview

Web Annotator is a Chrome Extension that lets you highlight text and add sticky-note annotations on any webpage. Your annotations persist across visits and can be exported for sharing or archiving. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

Web Annotator stores the following data locally on your device only using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API:

  • Annotations you create: the highlighted text selection, your note text, color, timestamp, and the URL of the page where the annotation was made
  • Your settings and preferences (default highlight color, font size, export format)
  • Your Pro license key (if applicable), stored locally to verify activation status

All annotations are stored exclusively on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • The full content of pages you annotate beyond the specific text selections you highlight
  • Browsing history of pages you have not annotated
  • Login credentials or form data

4. LemonSqueezy License Validation (Pro)

If you purchase a Pro license, your license key is sent to LemonSqueezy (our payment processor) solely to verify its validity. No other data — and crucially, none of your annotations — is included in this request. LemonSqueezy's privacy policy is available at lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

5. Permissions Explained

  • storage — saves your annotations and settings locally on your device
  • scripting / <all_urls> — injects the annotation overlay into pages so your highlights and notes are displayed when you revisit a page
  • contextMenus — adds "Annotate selection" to the right-click menu when you highlight text
  • downloads — exports your annotations as JSON, Markdown, or HTML to your device

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. The only external network call made by this extension is the LemonSqueezy license validation described in Section 4.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete individual annotations, clear all annotations for a specific page, or remove all annotation data from the extension settings panel. Uninstalling the extension deletes all stored data.

8. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
